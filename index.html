Seringai is a metal music group from Indonesia which was formed in 2002 consisting of Arian 13, Ricky Siahaan, Sammy Bramantyo, and Edy Khemod. Throughout his career, Seringai has released four albums, namely one mini album and three studio albums. Seringai began his career by releasing a mini album, entitled High Octane Rock, released in 2004. Seringai released their first album, entitled Wolf Militia, released in 2007. In 2010, Seringai released a documentary film, entitled Generasi Menolak Tua, containing the career journey of Seringai, directed by Sammy Bramantyo, in the form of a DVD box. Seringai released their second album, entitled Taring, which was released in 2012. In 2013, Seringai was the opening band at Metallica's concert in Jakarta. In the same year, Seringai released another maxi single, entitled Tragedi/Sang Lelaki, released in 2013 on vinyl. Seringai released their third album, entitled Like Fire, which was released on July 29 2018.

The music played by Seringai is well known to its fans as loud music that is synonymous with music outside the mainstream or underground. However, Seringai actually had a phase where they felt they didn't always want to be a metal band.According to Arian13's confession in a podcast, entitled "High Octane Podcast" he said:. "In the past, our musical direction wanted to try (like) At The Drive-In, post-hardcore."Apart from that, Seringai personnel admitted that Arian had thought about bringing music other than metal. Seringai is said to have thought about bringing post-hardcore nuanced music.
